An aeroplane is a fixed-wing aircraft with wings and engines that allow it to fly through the sky, making it a modern mode of transport for people and cargo. It is one of the fastest ways to travel long distances and relies on four forces—thrust, drag, weight, and lift—to fly, typically managed by a pilot or an advanced autopilot system
